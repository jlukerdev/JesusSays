### F-B3005 — WARNING

- **Teaching ID:** 14.1.3
- **Location:** Righteousness and Ethics > Salt and Light
- **Reference(s):** Matt 3:15 [P]
- **Check:** B3-Cat14-SubcategoryFit
- **Description:** Matt 3:15 ("it is fitting to fulfill all righteousness") is Jesus's statement to John the Baptist explaining why he submits to baptism. The primary content is a declaration about fulfilling all righteousness in the context of his baptism — it is not a teaching about the disciple's moral witness in the world (which is the subject of Cat 14.1, Salt and Light). Cat 14.1 is specifically Matt 5:13–16 scope. This teaching appears to have been assigned to 14.1 without a clear subcategory match. A stronger fit would be Cat 14.2 (The Law and Righteousness — Jesus's posture toward righteousness) or Cat 2.3 (His Divine Authority and Mission — completing the mission the Father gave).
- **Rule citation:** CLASSIFICATION_RULES.md §14.1 — Salt and Light scope is Matt 5:13–16 and the disciple's public moral witness.
- **Proposed action:** MOVE to Cat 14.2 (The Law and Righteousness) as the closest fit, since the teaching is Jesus's own statement about his role in fulfilling righteous requirements. Alternatively review for Cat 2.6 (Messianic Identity — fulfilling what was appointed).
- **Status:** OPEN

- **Proposed action:** MOVE — to Cat 16.1 (Power and Accountability of Words) since the primary punch line is "by your words you will be justified or condemned" (v37), which directly aligns with 16.1's scope. Cross-list in Cat 17.3 if the fruit-test aspect is also considered primary.

### F-B3004 — ERROR

- **Teaching ID:** 19.4.3
- **Location:** Marriage and Family > Care for Children
- **Reference(s):** Mark 9:39–41 [P]

- **Description:** Mark 9:39–41 is Jesus's response to John's report that disciples tried to stop an unknown exorcist: "Do not stop him… whoever is not against us is for us… whoever gives you a cup of water in my name will not lose his reward." The correct category is Cat 26 (Mission and Witness) or Cat 25 (The Church — who belongs) — specifically Cat 26.1 (Mission Given to the Disciples) or Cat 25.3 (Corporate Identity/Presence).
- **Rule citation:** CLASSIFICATION_RULES.md §19.4 — "Teachings about protecting, welcoming, and not causing children to stumble." Mark 9:39–41 concerns none of these subjects.
- **Proposed action:** MOVE to Cat 26.1 (Mission Scope and Method) as its primary theme is who can legitimately act in Jesus's name. If mission scope is not the primary frame, consider Cat 25 (The Church). Renumber.
- **Status:** OPEN
